Lamb Chops - Spiced with Mint-Mango Sauce

8 servings

Mint-Mango Sauce
1	one-pound ripe mangoes, peeled & seeded
1	cup fresh mint leaves
2	green onions, cut into 1-inch pieces
1	tablespoon fresh lime juice
1	serano chile, halved & seeded
Lamb Chops
2	tablespoons ground cumin
2	tablespoons ground coriander
1	tablespoon course kosher salt
2	teaspoons ground cinnamon
1	teaspoon turmeric
½	teaspoon cayenne pepper
½	teaspoon ground cardamom
¼	teaspoon ground cloves
2	garlic cloves
16	1½-inch thick lamb loin chops
Fresh mint sprigs

For the mint mango sauce. Blend all ingredients in a food processor until smooth. Transfer to a small bowl. Season with salt, cover, and chill. (Can be made one day ahead.)
For the lamb chops. Prepare barbecue (medium-high heat). Whisk first 8 ingredients in a small bowl. Mix oil and garlic in another bowl. Brush chops on both sides with oil mixture, and then press chops into the spice mixture to coat both sides. Grill chops to desired doneness, about 4 minutes per side for medium-rare. Transfer to a platter and garnish with mint sprigs. Serve warm or at room temperature with the mint-mango sauce.
