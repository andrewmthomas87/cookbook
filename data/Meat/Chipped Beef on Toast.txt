Pork Chop - Sauerkraut Bake

2 servings

8	ounces sauerkraut
1	tablespoon brown sugar, heaping
1	teaspoon salt
2	pork chops, 1-inch thick
½	teaspoon caraway seeds
¼	teaspoon allspice
¼	teaspoon cinnamon
salt and pepper, to taste
1	rib celery, thinly sliced
1	small onion, thinly sliced
1	tart apple, cored, peeled, thinly sliced
¼	cup ginger ale
¼	cup beer
1	tablespoon dry sherry

Preheat oven to 350F. Rinse and drain the sauerkraut; spread in the bottom of a shallow baking dish. Sprinkle with brown sugar and seasoned salt. Place pork chops on top of sauerkraut in a single layer. Sprinkle meat with caraway, allspice, cinnamon and salt and pepper. Top with celery, onion and apples. Pour beer and ginger ale over all; cover and bake for 1 hour. Remove cover and sprinkle with the sherry; bake for another 30 minutes, or until chops are nicely browned.
