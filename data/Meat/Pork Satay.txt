Pork Satay

4-6 servings

2	one pound pork tenderloins
½	cup chicken broth
¼	cup creamy peanut butter
¼	cup honey
¼	cup hoisin sauce
2	tablespoons minced cilantro
2	tablespoons soy sauce
1	teaspoons dark sesame oil
½	teaspoon crushed red pepper
1	clove garlic, minced
¼	cup water for later use

Cut each tenderloin lengthwise into equal strips about ½ “ thick. Place the pork strips in a shallow dish. Combine broth and next 8 ingredients Stir well with a wire whisk Reserve ¾ cup peanut butter mixture, set aside Add water to remaining peanut butter mixture Pour over the apork strips, turning to coat Marinate for 1-2 hours or longer. Remove pork strips from marinade, reserving marinade.
Grill about 6 minutes on each side or until done, basting occasionally with reserved marinade
Serve the pork strips with reserved peanut butter sauce.
