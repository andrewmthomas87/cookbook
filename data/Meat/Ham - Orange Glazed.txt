Ham - Orange Glazed

10-12 servings

1	boneless canned or fully cooked smoked ham, about 4 pounds
1	jar (10 ounces) orange marmalade
½	cup orange juice
1	tablespoon prepared mustard
¼	teaspoon ground cloves
¼	teaspoon ground ginger
½	cup raisins

Place ham on a rack in roasting pan, fat side up; score fat. Bake at 325F for 1 hour and 15 minutes. In a saucepan over low heat combine marmalade, orange juice, mustard, cloves, and ginger. Simmer for 5 minutes.
Spoon half of the orange mixture over the ham; continue baking for 30 minutes. Add raisins to the remaining orange mixture and serve with ham.
