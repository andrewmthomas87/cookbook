Flank Steak - Grilled with Poblanos & Onions

4 servings

1	teaspoon ground cumin
1	teaspoon coarse salt
½	teaspoon paprika
¼	teaspoon freshly ground pepper
¼	cup chopped cilantro
1	flank steak, about 1½ pounds
2	poblano chilies
1	red onion
1	tablespoon olive oil

Mix the cumin, salt, paprika, pepper and 2 tablespoons of the cilantro in a small bowl. Rub the mixture over both sides of the steak. Set aside.
Heat a grill or grill pan for medium-high heat. Stem and seed the chilies; cut into strips. Cut the onion in half and slice into 1/4-inch slices. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet. Add the chilies and onions; cook, stirring over high heat, until crisp-tender, about 5 minutes, or to desired doneness. Set aside.
Grill the steak to medium rare, about 7 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. Transfer to a cutting board; let sit 5 minutes. Slice thinly on the diagonal. Divide meat among 4 plates. Top with chilies and onions; sprinkle with remaining cilantro.
