Beef & Sausage Burgers

6 servings

1	pound lean ground beef
1	pound hot or mild Italian sausage, removed from casings
1	small onion, finely chopped
½	small red bell pepper, finely chopped
2	teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
½	teaspoon salt
Freshly ground pepper
6	hamburger buns

Heat a grill or grill pan to medium-high. Lightly mix together the ground beef, sausage, onion, bell pepper and Worcestershire sauce in a medium bowl; form into 6 patties. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Cook, turning once, to desired degree of doneness, or 8-10 minutes for medium-rare. Serve on buns.
