Beef Tenderloin – Marinated

6-8 servings

1	whole beef tenderloin (4-6 pounds) cleaned & tied
1	cup extra virgin olive oil
1	cup dry white wine
¼	cup fresh lime juice
3	garlic cloves, sliced
Salt & pepper

Preheat oven to 400F. Trim fat and tendons from the beef tenderloin, reserving side piece and the thinnest part of the tail end (about 4 inches) for beef stir-fry. Double tail end back and tie with twine. Marinate main for one hour at room temperature, then place in oven about 20-25 minutes before desired serving time, and roast to internal temperature of 125F. Let set for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
