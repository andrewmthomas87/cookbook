Lamb - Honeyed Leg

6 servings

1	small leg of lamb, about 5 pounds
½	cup hot water
⅓	cup honey
1	cup soy sauce
½	cup dry white wine
mint jelly for serving

Preheat oven to 375F.
Remove fat from lamb to avoid strong taste. Mix the honey, soy sauce and wine together in a small bowl; then pour mixture over lamb and marinate 24 hours turning several times. Pour ½ cup of water over before cooking.
Roast in the oven for about 35 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 110F. Baste the lamb with the mixture; then roast for an additional 20 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 128F.
Remove from the oven, cover with aluminum foil and rest for 10-15 minutes; then slice and serve with mint jelly.
