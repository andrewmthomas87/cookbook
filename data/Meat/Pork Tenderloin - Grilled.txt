Pork Tenderloin - Grilled

4 to 6 servings

2	one pound pork tenderloins, brined for 30 minutes ()
4	to 6 garlic cloves, sliced
3	tablespoons olive oil
3	tablespoons chopped rosemary leaves
Several sprigs of fresh rosemary for garnish

Brine the pork tenderloins for 30 minutes in Â¼ cup of salt in 2 quarts of water. Remove and dry, then rub with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and chopped rosemary leaves.
Insert slices of the garlic about every inch or so around the pork tenderloins.
Grill for 15-20 minutes until internal temperature is 140F Slice and serve while hot.
