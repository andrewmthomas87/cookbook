Beef Tenderloin with Garlic-Mustard

8 servings

2	pounds beef tenderloin cu
4	cloves garlic, finely chopped
¼	cup grainy mustard
2	tablespoon Dijon mustard
2	teaspoons Spanish paprika
¼	teaspoon kosher salt
¼	teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1	tablespoon low-sodium soy sauce
2	tablespoons white wine vinegar
1	tablespoon honey

Heat grill to high. Brush the meat liberally on both sides with the Garlic-Mustard Glaze. Grill the meat for 2 to 3 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked to medium-rare doneness, brushing with the remaining glaze while grilling. Remove from grill and serve.
