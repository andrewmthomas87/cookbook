Ham - Andalusian with Noodles

6 servings

1	tablespoon olive oil
1	small red onion, thinly sliced
2	cups slivered cooked ham
1	can chicken broth (14 ounces)
Zest and juice of 1 orange
1	tablespoon sherry vinegar
2	teaspoons cornstarch
1	cup pitted green olives, coarsely chopped
Â½	cup chopped smoked almonds
Chopped parsley
12	ounces egg noodles or fettuccine

Heat a stockpot of water to a boil; cook noodles or fettuccini. Drain. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add the onion; cook, stirring, until lightly browned, about 8 minutes Add the ham; cook, stirring, about 2 minutes. Add the broth, zest, and orange juice.
Mix vinegar and cornstarch in a small bowl; stir into skillet. Cook until slightly thickened, 1 minute. Stir in the olives and almonds. Place pasta in a large serving bowl and pour the sauce over. Toss to mix; sprinkle with the parsley.
