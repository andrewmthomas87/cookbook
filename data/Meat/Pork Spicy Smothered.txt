Pork Spicy Smothered

4 servings

2	tablespoons olive oil
1	small onion, sliced
3	cloves garlic, minced
4	boneless pork chops
½	teaspoon salt
3	tablespoons spicy or sweet paprika
¼	teaspoon hot pepper flakes or to taste
2	tablespoons sherry or red wine vinegar
1	can (15 ounces) stewed tomatoes

Brine the pork tenderloins for 30 minutes in 2 quarts of water with ½ cup salt and ¼ cup sugar.
Heat oil in large heavy skillet over high heat; add onion. Stir-fry until wilted, 3 minutes. Add garlic; stir-fry 1 minute. Transfer onion and garlic to a plate.
Salt the pork. Add to the skillet; brown, 3 minutes per side. Remove pork to a plate. Reduce heat to medium; add paprika and pepper flakes to skillet. Stir in onions and garlic; cook 2 minutes. Add vinegar; cook, stirring, 1 minute. Stir in tomatoes. Cover; cook 3 minutes. Return pork to pan; cover with sauce. Cook until pork is no longer pink in center, 5 minutes
