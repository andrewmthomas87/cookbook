Hamburgers – Asian with Shitake Mushrooms

Makes 4 servings

2	pounds ground sirloin
2	small jalapeno peppers, stemmed, seeded, minced
2	to 4 tablespoons minced garlic
2½	tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro leaves
2	teaspoons thinly sliced green onions
1	tablespoon each: curry powder, ground cumin, sugar
½	teaspoon salt, freshly ground pepper
1	bunches green onions, cut into 3 inch pieces
8	fresh shitake mushrooms, stemmed
3	tablespoons olive oil
¼	 cup bottled teriyaki sauce, plus more if desired

Lightly combine beef, chilies, garlic, cilantro, sliced green onions, curry powder, cumin, sugar, salt, and pepper to taste, in a mixing bowl. Form mixture into 4 equal patties approximately ¾ inch thick. Cover with plastic wrap; refrigerate until ready to cook.
Prepare a charcoal or gas grill for direct cooking. Combine long green onions and mushrooms; drizzle with 2 tablespoons of the olive oil and toss. Brush grill grate with the remaining tablespoon of olive oil to prevent sticking.
Place burgers on the grill. Cook 4-5 minutes on one side; turn. Add the mushrooms & long green onions using a grill basket. Brush the burgers and the vegetables with teriyaki sauce. Cook burgers 4 minutes on second side for medium. Remove burgers from grill, place on a bed of lettuce and top with the long green onions and mushrooms.
