Lamb Stew with Rice

4 servings

Stew
2	to3 pounds of lamb shoulder with fat
trimmed off, then cut into 1 inch cubes
3	large onions diced coarsely
1	tablespoons olive oil
1	small can of tomato paste (6 oz)
1	tablespoon cumin
1	tablespoon ground coriander
1	teaspoon ground allspice
2	chicken bouillon cubes issolved in ½ cup of warm water.
Rice
2	cups long grain white rice
2	tablespoons olive oil
1	large onion diced finely
4	cups chicken broth
½	teaspoon red pepper flakes
Grated rind of one large orange
¼	cup piñola (pine nuts)
1	carrot, grated
¼	cup dried cranberries

Stew: Brown lamb cubes in olive oil over high heat and set aside. Sauté onions in olive oil until translucent (2-3 minutes), and then add spices and tomato paste, followed by the chicken bouillon. Place all ingredients in a deep baking pan and bake uncovered at 350F for 45 minutes.
Rice: Sauté diced onion in olive oil until translucent. Add the rice, stirring well to coat with olive oil. Add chicken broth, bring to a slow boil, then reduce heat, cover d and simmer for 15 minutes without uncovering. Before serving stir in grated orange peel, grated carrot, pine nuts and dried cranberries.
