Sausage & Egg Casserole

8 servings

1	pound Italian sweet sausage, casings removed
1	small red bell pepper, chopped
½	cup chopped shallots
½	cup chopped drained oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes
2	garlic cloves, minced
tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
6	large eggs
1	cup half and half
cup whipping cream
cups grated mozzarella cheese
½	teaspoon salt
Freshly ground black pepper

Preheat oven to 375F
Butter a 13 x 9-inch glass baking dish.
Sauté sausage until brown and cooked through, breaking it up with the back of a fork into small pieces, about 10 minutes. Add pepper, shallots and garlic and sauté 3 minutes. Add sun-dried tomatoes and 2 tablespoons parsley. Stir 1 minute.
Spread sausage mixture in prepared dish. Whisk together, eggs, egg yolks, half and half, whipping cream, 1½ cups cheese and salt and pepper in a large bowl. Pour egg mixture over sausage mixture. Sprinkle remaining cheese and 2 tablespoons parsley over top.
Bake until top of casserole is golden brown and knife inserted into center comes out clean, about 30 minutes.
Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
