Spinach Quiche without Crust

4-8 servings

1	ten-ounce package of frozen spinach, squeezed dry
8	ounces Swiss cheese, grated
4	slices day-old French bread torn into small pieces
6	eggs. lightly beaten
¼	cup chopped onions
1	tablespoon butter
1½	teaspoons nutmeg

Preheat oven to 350F. Mix spinach, cheese, bread, and eggs together. Sauté onions in butter, then add to spinach mixture. Season mixture with nutmeg and salt and pepper. Transfer to a greased pie plate. Bake for 30 minutes or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean. Cut into wedges and serve immediately.
