Pancakes – Dutch Baby

10-12 servings

4	eggs
1	cup flour
1	cup milk
1	stick butter
½	teaspoon salt
2	teaspoons sugar
1	teaspoon ground cinnamon

Preheat oven to 450F.
Heat large iron skillet on burner, then melt butter, swirling around to coat sides.
Mix eggs and milk, add salt, then flour, beating gently, leaving some lumps.
Pour mixture in skillet, place in oven and bake for 15 minutes, turning once to ensure even baking. Sprinkle with the cinnamon-sugar mixture and serve with warm syrup and/or honey.
Variation: add diced apples to mixture before baking.
