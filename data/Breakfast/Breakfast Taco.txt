Breakfast Taco

4 servings

4	large eggs
4	large egg whites
coarse salt and ground pepper 4	six-inch corn tortillas
4	teaspoons olive oil
4	scallions, thinly sliced
4	tablespoons red salsa
4	tablespoons sour cream

Beat the eggs and the egg whites in a small bowl with 1 teaspoon salt and Â½ teaspoon pepper. Toast the tortillas in the oven and set aside.
Heat the olive oil and scallions in a small non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cook scallions until soft, about 1 minute. Add the egg mixture and cook, stirring constantly with a heatproof rubber spatula or wooden spoon until set, 1 to 2 minutes.
Spoon one fourth of the egg mixture into each of the toasted tortillas and top with salsa and sour cream; fold the tortilla over and serve immediately.
