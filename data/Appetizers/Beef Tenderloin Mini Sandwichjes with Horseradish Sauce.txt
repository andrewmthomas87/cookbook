Beef Tenderloin Mini Sandwiches with Horseradish Sauce

20 Appetizers

1	sour cream
¼	cup Dijon mustard
2	tablespoons prepared horseradish
1	three pound beef tenderloin roasted medium-rare
2	cups arugula leaves
2	thin baguettes, sliced ¼ inch thick

Combine first 3 ingredients, stirring well with a whisk. Cover and chill.
Arrange 1 or 2 arugula leaves evenly on each bread slice. Place 1 beef slice and about 1 tablespoon chilled sauce over each bread slice.
