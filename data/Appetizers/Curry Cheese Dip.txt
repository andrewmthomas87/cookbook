Curry Cheese Dip

24 servings

8	ounces cream cheese
2	tablespoons sour cream
2	teaspoons curry powder
½	cup chopped green onions
½	cup golden raisins
½	cup coarse chopped peanuts
1	cup mango chutney

Mix all ingredients fully. Refrigerate up to 4 days.
Top with options: bacon pieces or coconut flakes.
Serve with crackers
