Guacamole

2 cups

4	fresh avocados, mashed gently
3	plum tomatoes, seeded & chopped
½	cup chopped red onion
¼	cup chopped cilantro
1	teaspoon salt
2	tablespoons fresh lime juice
2	tablespoons chopped garlic

In a medium bowl, combine the mashed avocados, chopped tomatoes & onion, the chopped cilantro, salt, lime juice, and chopped garlic. Fold all the ingredients together gently with a fork until completely mixed. Serve with Trader Joes Corn Scoops.
