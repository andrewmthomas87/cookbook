Meatballs - Swedish with Creamy Dill Sauce

4 servings

1	pound ground veal
⅔	cup fresh white breadcrumbs
⅔	 cup onion; minced
2	egg whites
4	tablespoon fresh dill; minced
2	teaspoons vegetable oil
⅔	cup half and half
½	cup whipping cream

Line a small baking pan with waxed paper. Combine the veal, breadcrumbs, onion, egg white and 3 tablespoons dill in large bowl and blend well. Shape veal mixture into about 32 balls and place on prepared pan. (Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Heat oil in medium nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add meatballs to skillet; cook until brown and cooked through, turning frequently, about 12 minutes. Add half and half, whipping cream and 2 tablespoons dill to skillet; stir until sauce is slightly thickened, scraping up any browned bits, about 3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
