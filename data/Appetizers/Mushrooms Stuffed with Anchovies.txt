Mushrooms Stuffed with Anchovies

15-20 Appetizers

½	cup bread, crusts removed and crumbled
¼	cup milk
1	pound button mushroom, medium size
4	slices bacon, cooked and finely chopped
4	anchovy fillets, finely chopped
1	egg, beaten
¼	cup dry breadcrumbs
¼	cup olive oil

Preheat oven to 400F. Cover a large baking sheet with aluminum foil, then grease with a bit of olive oil.
Soak the bread pieces in milk in a small bowl.
Remove the mushroom stems and chop finely. Put the chopped stems into a bowl with the bacon pieces, anchovies, garlic, egg, salt and pepper.
Squeeze soaked bread to remove excess milk; add to bacon mixture and mix well.
Divide bread mixture among mushrooms, piling mixture into small mounds.
Sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
Bake 20-30 minutes, until top of stuffing is crisp.
