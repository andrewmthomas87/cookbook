Shrimp Crescents

32 appetizers

1	can (14 ounce) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1	can (4 ounce) diced green chilies, drained
1	cup grated Parmesan cheese
¼	cup mayonnaise
¼	teaspoon ground red pepper
¼	teaspoon finely minced garlic
2	cans (8 ounce each) refrigerated crescent dinner rolls
32	cooked small shrimp, peeled and deveined

Preheat the oven to 375F.
In bowl, combine all ingredients except crescent rolls and shrimp. Mix well.
Unroll crescent rolls and cut each triangle in half lengthwise, forming 32 triangles. Stretch dough to shape and flatten.
Spoon a rounded tablespoon of artichoke mixture onto each triangle and place 1 shrimp on top. Roll up, starting at wide and place on well greased baking sheet.
At this point crescents may be refrigerated until ready to bake and serve.
Bake at 375F for 12 to 15 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.
