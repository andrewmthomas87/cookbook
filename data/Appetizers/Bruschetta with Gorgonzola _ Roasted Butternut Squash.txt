Bruschetta with Gorgonzola & Roasted Butternut Squash

12 servings

2	pounds butternut pumpkin (unpeeled weight)
Olive Oil
Sea salt & freshly ground pepper
1	loaf of French bread cut into twelve
½-inch slices
8	ounces gorgonzola cheese
12	oregano leaves

Preheat oven to 375F. Peel butternut squash, remove seeds and dice flesh into ½ inch chunks. Place on an oven tray. Drizzle with oil and add a good sprinkle of salt and pepper. Roast 15 minutes or until tender and starting to brown.
Toast bread and brush lightly with olive oil. Spread with gorgonzola. Top with pumpkin. Place under the broiler until the cheese just begins to melt. Drizzle over a little more oil and serve garnished with fresh oregano laves.
