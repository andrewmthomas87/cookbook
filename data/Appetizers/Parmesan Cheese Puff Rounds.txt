Parmesan Cheese Puff Rounds

50-60 appetizers

8	ounces cream cheese, softened
3	tablespoons mayonnaise
3	scallions, finely chopped, including green tops
3	drops Tabasco sauce
⅔	cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
Thinly sliced white bread
butter

In a food processor, mix the cream cheese, mayonnaise, onions, and Tabasco. Transfer to a bowl, stir in ½ cup of the Parmesan cheese, and chill until ready to serve
Cut out 50-60 1½ inch bread rounds and butter on one side. Place on a tray and broil until browned. Turn over and lightly brown the unbuttered side and spread with the cheese mixture, forming a point in the middle. Dip in the remaining Parmesan cheese. Place on a baking sheet and broil until puffy and light brown
