Bruschetta with Baby Tomatoes & Peppers

12 servings

½	cup sun dried tomatoes
2	tablespoons tomato paste
1	tablespoon olive oil
1	tablespoon brown sugar
Sea salt & ground black pepper
2	yellow bell peppers
36	cherry or grape tomatoes
Extra olive oil for drizzling & for basting bread
1	loaf of French bread cut into 12 ¾ inch thick slices
12	basil leaves

Soak sun dried tomatoes in lukewarm water for 15 minutes. Pat dry. Place in a food processor with tomato paste, oil, sugar, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Blend into a smooth paste.
Preheat oven to 375F. Place whole peppers on a baking tray and roast until skins brown. Set aside to cool. Place tomatoes on tray and drizzle with oil. Roast until skins start to char. Peel peppers and cut into 1 inch pieces (discard seeds). Place on tray with the tomatoes, drizzle with oil and keep warm.
Toast French bread slices and brush lightly with oil. Spread with the tomato mixture. Place 3 roasted baby tomatoes and some pepper on each slice. Drizzle over a little more olive oil and top with a basil leaf.
