Calzone with Prosciutto & Double Cheese

4 servings

2	cups grated mozzarella cheese
4	ounces soft fresh goat cheese, crumbled
2	ounces prosciutto slices, chopped
3	teaspoons chopped fresh thyme
2	garlic cloves, smashed & minced
Extra virgin olive oil
1	Pillsbury tubular pizza dough

Position rack in bottom third of oven; pre-heat to 425F.
Lightly oil a 12”x13” baking sheet. Unroll the dough on to floured surface and sprinkle with flour. Roll out to fill the baking sheet and stretch to all four edges, leaving a bit of extra dough around the edges. Brush lightly with olive oil.
Toss the first five ingredients in a medium bowl. Season with pepper. Spread the filling on half of the dough, leaving a one-inch border on the sides. Fold the other half of the dough over. Crimp the edges to seal. Bake until puffed and brown, about 18 minutes. Transfer to cutting board and brush with olive oil, then cut into appetizer squares with pizza cutter and serve on tray.
