Salsa - Sallys

appetizers for 6-8 people.

4	16 oz cans chopped tomatoes
1	small bell pepper
1	bunch green onions
2	sticks of celery
½	of one 4 ounce can jalapenos (may be adjusted as needed)
1	teaspoon black pepper
1	teaspoon salt
2	cloves garlic
¼	cup vinegar
¼	cup olive oil
¼	teaspoon minced garlic

Mix well then put in food processor. Do not mutilate, make as chunky as you like. Serve with tostado chips.

May 1999
