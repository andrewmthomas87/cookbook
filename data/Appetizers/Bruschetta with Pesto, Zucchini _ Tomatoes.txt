Bruschetta with Pesto, Zucchini & Tomatoes

12 servings

1	tablespoon olive oil
4	tomatoes, sliced thin
2	zucchini, peeled & sliced thin
½	cup pesto
extra virgin olive oil for brushing bread & drizzling
1	loaf of French bread cut into 12 ¾ inch thick slices

Preheat oven to 375F.
Toast French bread slices and brush lightly with olive oil. Spread lightly with the pesto. Place 3 zucchini slices interleaved with 3 slices of tomatoes on top of the pesto. Drizzle over a little more olive oil and top and warm in the oven, then serve.
