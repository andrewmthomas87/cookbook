Honey Smoked Salmon on Naan

20 Appetizers

1	package Honey Smoked Salmon
2	pieces of naan cut into 1½ inch squares
3	tablespoons prepared horseradish
½	cup mayonnaise
2	cups arugula for garnish

Warm naan in a 200F oven for 5 minutes, then cut into 1½ inch squares.
Remove skin from salmon and place on a serving platter alongside the naan slices with a small knife for serving.
Mix horseradish and mayonnaise to taste. Serve in a small bowl and set next to salmon
