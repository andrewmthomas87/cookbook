Asparagus with Tarragon Cream

100 appetizers

4	pounds young tender asparagus
1	medium bunches fresh tarragon
1	pint sour cream
Salt, pepper, tarragon vinegar to taste

Blanch the asparagus in a large kettle of boiling water. Cook until just tender (2 to 4 minutes). Plunge immediately into iced water, drain, and chill.
Strip the leaves of tarragon from the stems. Mince finely. Mix into the sour cream; add the salt, pepper, and vinegar to taste.
To serve, put the cream in a bowl set into the center of a large tray. Arrange the vegetables neatly around the bowl. Guests will pick up a stem and dip it into the sauce.
