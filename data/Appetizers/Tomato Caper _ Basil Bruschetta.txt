Tomato Caper & Basil Bruschetta

4 servings

1½	cups diced seeded peeled tomatoes (about 2 large)
¼	cup coarsely chopped fresh basil
1	tablespoon drained capers
1	tablespoon olive oil
4	large ½-inch thick slices French baguette
1	large garlic clove, peeled, cut in half crosswise

Mix tomatoes, basil, capers and oil in a bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Lightly toast bread on both sides under broiler. Rub one side of each bread slice with the cut side of garlic. Spoon tomato mixture over, dividing equally. Arrange bruschetta on a platter and serve.
