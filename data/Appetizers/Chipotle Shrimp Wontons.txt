Chipotle Shrimp Wontons

24 pieces

Olive oil for muffin cups
1	sixteen ounce package of frozen wonton skins (round)
½	pound shrimp, cooked, peeled, and coarsely chopped
1	yellow bell pepper, roasted, peeled, and chopped
1	red bell pepper, roasted, peeled, and chopped
½	cup chopped fresh cilantro
1	finely chopped jalapeño pepper
8	ounces Fontina cheese, shredded

Preheat oven to 350F. Brush mini-muffin cups with olive oil. Press 1 wonton skin into each cup. Bake about 10 minutes until tops are golden brown. Remove from oven and cool.
In a large bowl, combine shrimp, roasted & chopped bell peppers, cilantro, jalapeño pepper, and the Fontina cheese. Remove cooled skins from the muffin pan and place on a baking sheet. Fill each skin with the shrimp mixture and bake 7-10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
