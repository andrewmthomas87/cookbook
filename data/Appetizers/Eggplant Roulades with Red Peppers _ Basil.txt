Eggplant Roulades with Red Peppers & Basil

8 servings

2	large eggplants
½ cup safflower oil
1	pound soft Mozzarella cheese
¾	cup grated Parmesan cheese
8	fresh basil leaves

Preheat oven to 350F. Cut eggplants horizontally into ¼ inch thick slices, approximately eight slices. Sauté eggplant slices in one layer in oil over medium heat, turning to brown on both sides. Remove slices to paper towel and soak excess oil off top with another paper towel.
Cut 1 ounce pieces of Mozzarella cheese and place one on each soft eggplant slice. Follow with a layer of roasted red bell pepper, then prinkle with Parmesan cheese. Roll eggplant slices around cheese and stack vertically in a medium baking dish. Heat for 10 minutes in oven, then sprinkle with more Parmesan cheese and top each with a fresh basil leaf. Serve warm.
