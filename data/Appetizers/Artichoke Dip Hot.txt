Artichoke Dip Hot

3 cups

1	eight ounce can of artichoke hearts packed in water
1	seven ounce can of chopped green chilis, mild
1	cup grated Parmesan cheese
1	cup mayonnaise

Preheat oven to 350F. Combine all of the ingredients. Place in an ovenproof serving dish. Bake for 30 minutes, until slightly browned around the edges. Let rest for 10-15 minutes, then serve with Tostito or Dorita chips for dipping. Small knives are handy for spreading the dip on the chips.
