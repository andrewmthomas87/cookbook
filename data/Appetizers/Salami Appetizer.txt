Salami Appetizer

25 appetizers

3	ounces cream cheese, softened
2	tablespoons mayonnaise
2	tablespoons chopped chives or scallions (green parts)
1	dash Tabasco
25	thin slices salami

Blend softened cream cheese, mayonnaise, chives, and Tabasco.
Place 1 teaspoon of the cheese mixture on a slice of salami, then roll into a cone and secure with a toothpick. Refrigerate until ready to serve to set the cheese.
