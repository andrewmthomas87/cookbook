Asparagus Roasted and wrapped in Prosciutto

48 appetizers

48	spears of asparagus (about 2 pounds)
Olive Oil
16 slices of prosciutto

Trim and blanch asparagus spears for 3 minutes in boiling water. Remove quickly and plunge into ice water to stop cooking.
Wrap each asparagus spear in prosciutto and arrange flat on a cookie sheet.  Drizzle, with olive oil., then place under broiler for about 3 minutes or until prosciutto begins to crisp.
Serve as an appetizer
