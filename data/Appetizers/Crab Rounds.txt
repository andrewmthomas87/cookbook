Crab Rounds

50-55 pieces

1	6½ ounce can crabmeat, drained
1	cup mayonnaise
½	cup grated onion
1	cup shredded cheddar cheese
6	drops Tabasco sauce
¼	teaspoon curry powder
1	French bread baguettes, sliced ½ inch thick.

In a medium bowl, combine crabmeat, mayonnaise, grated onion, cheddar cheese, Tabasco, and curry powder. This may be made one day in advance. To serve, place mixture on bread rounds and broil until golden brown. Serve immediately.

May 1998
