Mushrooms with Blue Cheese - Warm

6 servings

1	pound fresh chanterelle mushrooms (½ pound each of oyster & shitake can be used)
2	slices of Italian pancetta bacon, chopped coarsely
3	large shallots, thinly sliced
4	tablespoons olive oil
2	tablespoons Sherry wine vinegar
Salt & pepper
½	cup blue cheese

Heat oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the pancetta and shallots; sauté until beginning to brown, about 5 minutes. Add 2 more tablespoons of olive oil, then the mushrooms. Sprinkle with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Add 1 tablespoon Sherry wine vinegar and boil until evaporated, stirring constantly. Remove from heat
Pour a 4-5 ounce bag of prepared Marché or Arugula in a large mixing bowl. Add 2 tablespoons olive oil and toss. Add 1 tablespoon Sherry wine vinegar, season with salt and pepper and toss again.
Divide the greens into 6 serving bowls, and then top each with a serving of the mushroom mixture. Sprinkle with crumbled Maytag blue cheese and serve.
