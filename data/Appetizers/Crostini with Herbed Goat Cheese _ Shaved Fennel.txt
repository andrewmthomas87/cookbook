Crostini with Herbed Goat Cheese & Fennel

8 servings

Â¾	pound herbed goat cheese cheese
1	cup shaved fennel
1	loaf Italian bread, sliced
Olive oil
Coarse salt and freshly ground black pepper

Shave one fennel bulb thinly. Break up cheese and add along with fennel. Mix with a fork or fingers.
Cut largest bread slices in two. Brush two rimmed baking sheets with olive oil. Arrange bread over olive oil. Spread each slice with about 1 tablespoon of the cheese and fennel mixture. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Slide into a 350F-degree oven and bake until cheese has melted, about 15 minutes.
