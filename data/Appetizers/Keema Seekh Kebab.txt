Keema Seekh Kebab

4-6 servings or 15-20 appetizers

1	pound minced lamb or beef
2	tablespoons besan (chick pea flour)
1	medium onion, finely diced
1	clove of garlic, crushed
2	tablespoons finely chopped fresh cilantro
1	teaspoon finely grated ginger root
1	teaspoon salt
1	teaspoon garam masala*
Â¼	cup yoghurt

Combine minced meat with all other ingredients except the yoghurt. Mix thoroughly and knead well until the mixture becomes very smooth. Form portions of the mixture into sausage shapes around skewers that have been soaked in water. Beat the yoghurt slightly and coat the meat with it, then place over barbecue or under broiler until browned on all sides and cooked through. Serve as an appetizer with sauces such as cilantro or horseradish.
*Garam Masala can be made by grinding cumin seeds, coriander seeds, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, black peppercorns and nutmeg in a food processor.
