Shrimp Cocktail With Herb-Chili Sauce

Serves 8

1/3 cup olive oil
¼	cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
¼	cup chopped fresh basil leaves
¼	cup chopped fresh chives
½	jalapeño pepper, chopped
1	tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1	clove garlic, chopped
kosher salt and black pepper
1½	cooked peeled and deveined large shrimp

Combine the oil, cilantro, basil, chives, chili, lemon juice, garlic, ½ teaspoon salt, and ¼ teaspoon pepper in a medium bowl. Serve with the shrimp.
