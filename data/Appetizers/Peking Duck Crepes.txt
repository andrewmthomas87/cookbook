Peking Duck Crepes

20 Appetizers

½	cup plain flour
¼	cup water
¼	cup milk
2	eggs
2	tablespoons butter, melted
1	Peking duck (see note)
6	scallions, cut to 2 inches
½	cup hoisin sauce

Combine flour, cornflour, water, milk, eggs and half the butter in a food processor. Process until batter is smooth. Cover and stand for 15 minutes.
Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Brush with remaining butter. Pour a tablespoonful of batter into frying pan. Spread to form a thin pancake, about 3 inches in diameter. Cook for 2 minutes. Turn and cook for a further 1 minute. Transfer to a plate. Repeat with remaining batter.
Remove skin and meat from duck. Thinly slice both skin and meat. Cut onions into 10cm lengths.
Place some duck skin and meat, 2 pieces of scallion and 1 teaspoon of hoisin sauce onto each pancake. Roll up. Serve.
