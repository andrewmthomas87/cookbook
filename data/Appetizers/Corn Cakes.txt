Corn Cakes

24 Appetizers

1½ cup yellow cornmeal
¼ cup all purpose flour
1	teaspoon baking soda
1	teaspoon sugar
1	teaspoon salt
2	cups buttermilk
2	tablespoons cooking oil
1	egg separated with white beaten
1	cup cubed cheddar cheese

Preheat a special Nordic ware pancake pan, then brush with olive oil. Pour 1 tablespoon of the cornmeal pancake batter into each pan well, then place one cube of cheddar cheese in the center of each. Top each with another tablespoon of batter and cook until golden brown and crispy – 5 to 6 minutes. Flip the pancakes over and cook another 3-4 minutes longer.
Can also be filled with fruit or chocolate.
