Bruschetta with Broiled Eggplant & Goat Cheese

12 pieces

1	large eggplant
Sea Salt
Olive oil for basting & drizzling
Â½	cup goat cheese (Chevre)
1	tamarillo, cut into 12 crescent slices

Cut eggplant into 12 round slices. Sprinkle well with salt and let stand for 15 minutes. Rinse thoroughly and pat dry. Baste with olive oil and place under boiler 5-7 minutes until each side is lightly charred.
Place focaccia slices on a baking tray. Add a slice of eggplant to each and top with a thin slice of goat cheese. Broil about 5 minutes or until cheese starts to melt. Top with tamarillo slice and place back under the broiler for 30 seconds. Drizzle with a little more olive oil before serving.
