Asparagus Roasted in Olive Oil & Balsamic Vinegar

48 appetizers

48	spears of asparagus (about 2 pounds)
Olive Oil
Balsamic Vinegar

Trim and blanch asparagus spears for 3 minutes in boiling water. Remove quickly and plunge into ice water to stop cooking.
Arrange asparagus spears flat on a cookie sheet and drizzle, first with olive oil, then with balsamic vinegar
Place under broiler for about 3 minutes or until brown spots begin to appear.
Serve as an appetizer
