Pizza - Sicilian Zucchini

6 servings

1	ten ounce refrigerated pizza dough tube
Extra-virgin olive oil
½	 red onion, peeled & sliced ¼ inch thick
2	tablespoons olive oil, plus more for drizzling
Salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste
4	zucchini, sliced ¼ inch thick
Cornmeal for dusting
1	cup tomato sauce
2	cups grated provolone cheese
1	 cup grape tomatoes, halved
Grated Parmesan cheese for sprinkling
2	tablespoons thinly sliced fresh basil

Position rack in bottom third of oven; pre-heat to 425F. Lightly oil a cookie sheet and sprinkle with cornmeal. Unroll the dough on to a floured surface and sprinkle with flour. Roll out to fill the cookie sheet and stretch to all four edges, leaving a bit of extra dough around the edges. Brush lightly with olive oil; and bake for 5 minutes, or until lightly browned. Sprinkle the dough with olive oil again when it comes out.
In a bowl, stir together the onion, 1 tablespoon of the olive oil, salt and pepper. Sauté lightly in a skillet and transfer to a plate.
In the same bowl, stir together the zucchini, the remaining 1 tablespoon of the olive oil, salt and pepper. Sauté lightly in a skillet and transfer to a plate.
Top the partially baked dough with the tomato sauce, provolone, zucchini, onion and tomatoes. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese has melted and the crust is lightly browned, 15 to 20 minutes.
Transfer the pizza to a cutting board and let cool for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with the Parmesan cheese and basil and drizzle with olive oil. Cut into slices and serve immediately
