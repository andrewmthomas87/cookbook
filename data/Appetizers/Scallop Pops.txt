Scallop Pops

18 appetizers

1	orange
¼	cup maple syrup
1	teaspoon coarse salt
¼	teaspoon ground red pepper
1½	pounds large fresh sea scallops – 18 scallops
2	strips bacon
18	Lollipop sticks

Juice: Zest and juice orange. Stir together 1 teaspoon zest, ½ cup juice, maple syrup, ½ teaspoon salt and red pepper.
Slice: Cut thick scallops into discs ½-inch thick. Pat dry. Season with ½ teaspoon salt.
Crisp: Cut bacon strips crosswise into ½-inch tabs. Toss in a large skillet over medium heat until crisp, 10 minutes. Use a slotted spoon to lift out bacon. Drain on a paper-towel-lined plate. Munch on chips. Leave rendered fat in pan.
Sear: Heat bacon fat over medium-high heat. Dot with scallops and let sizzle (without moving) until nicely browned, 2 minutes. Flip and brown the other side. Use a spatula to lift scallops onto a plate.
Reduce: Carefully pour juice mixture into skillet. Stir over medium heat until thick, 5 minutes. Return scallops to pan and stir until sticky with sauce, 1 minute.
Serve: Skewer each scallop on a lollipop stick.
