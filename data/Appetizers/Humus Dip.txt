Humus Dip

about 6 servings

2	cans garbanzo beans
1	lemon (juice & zest)
5	garlic cloves, finely minced
½	cup tahini
½	cup good olive oil
¼	cup parsley, minced
sesame seeds, lightly toasted
Pita bread, cut into triangles
Crudities (celery, carrots, green & red bell peppers)

Blend the garbanzo beans in food processor; add lemon juice, tahini, garlic cloves, and olive oil. Spread in a fairly large flat bowl and top with minced parley, drizzled olive oil, and toasted sesame seeds.
