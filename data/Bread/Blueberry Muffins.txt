Blueberry Muffins

12 servings

1	cup milk
½	cup (1 stick) butter
1½	teaspoons grated orange peel
1	teaspoon vanilla extract
2	large eggs
2	cups all-purpose flour
¾	cup sugar
2½	teaspoons baking powder
¾	teaspoon salt
1½	cup fresh blueberries or frozen, unthawed

Preheat oven to 400F and line a muffin pan with 12 paper liners. Combine the first 4 ingredients in a heavy small saucepan. Stir over medium heat until the butter melts. Cool until the mixture is warm to the touch. Beat in the eggs.
Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a large bowl. Add milk mixture and stir just until blended. Fold in the blueberries. Divide the batter equally among the prepared cups. Bake until golden and tester inserted into the center of the muffins comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Transfer to a rack and cool.
