Sour Cream Coffeecake

1 loaf

Cake Ingredients
8	pecan halves
1	cup (2 sticks) butter
2	cups sugar
2	eggs
2	cups sifted cake flour
1	teaspoon baking powder
1	cup sour cream
½	teaspoon vanilla
Pinch of salt
Topping Ingredients
½	cup chopped pecans
2	tablespoons sugar
1	teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the Cake: Preheat oven to 350F. Butter a 9-cup Bundt pan, placing a little extra butter in each notch. Press a pecan half in each notch of the pan. Cream the butter and sugar in a large mixing bowl. Beat in the eggs. Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. Add gradually to the creamed mixture, blending well. Carefully fold in the sour cream and vanilla.
For the topping: Mix the chopped pecans with the sugar and cinnamon
Sprinkle 2 tablespoons of the topping on the bottom of the bundt pan then add one third of the cake batter. The alternate 2 tablespoons of the topping, another third of the batter, another 2 tablespoons of the topping, the final third of the batter, ending with the rest of the topping.
Bake for 55-60 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes in the pan, then turn out onto a serving plate. Cake will have a dark outer crust. Using margarine to coat the pan instead of butter will result in a lighter crust.
