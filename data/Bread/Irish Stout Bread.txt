Spoon Bread

one loaf

2	tablespoons oil
1	cup warm dark stout ale (such as Guinness or Whitneys)
⅓	cup molasses
⅓	cup chopped onions
1	tablespoon caraway seeds
2	cups bread flour
½	cup whole wheat flour
½	cup rye flour
½	cup cornmeal
2	teaspoons yeast

Add the ingredients to your machine according to the manufacturer's instructions.
Bake on a regular/white or regular/wheat cycle.
