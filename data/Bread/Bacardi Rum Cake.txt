Bacardi Rum Cake

1 loaf

Cake Ingredients
1	cup chopped pecans or walnuts
1	package (18.5-ounce) yellow cake mix
1	package (3.75-ounce) instant vanilla pudding mix
4	eggs
½	cup cold water
½	cup vegetable oil
1	teaspoon baking soda
½	cup Bacardi Dark rum
Glaze Ingredients
2	tablespoons butter
2	tablespoons water
½	cup granulated sugar
¼	cup Bacardi Dark rum

For the Cake: Preheat oven to 325F. Grease and flour a 12-cup Bundt pan. Sprinkle nuts over the bottom of the pan. Mix all cake ingredients together and beat about 5 minutes with an electric mixer at medium speed. Pour batter over the nuts in the pan. Bake for one hour. Set on a rack to cool. Invert on a serving plate. Prick the top of the cake; drizzle and brush the glaze evenly over the top and sides.
For the Glaze: Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in the water and sugar. Boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in rum.
