Easy Rolls

12 rolls

2	cups self-rising flour
1	cup milk
2	tablespoons sugar
4	heaping tablespoons mayonnaise
Butter, for greasing muffing tins

Preheat the oven to 350F.
Lightly grease a 12-cup muffin tin with butter.
In a large bowl, combine flour and milk, blending with a rubber spatula. Add the sugar and mayonnaise, and stir until just combined. Spoon batter into a prepared muffin tin. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, or until golden brown.
