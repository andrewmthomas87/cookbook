Green Chili Corn Bread

12 servings

¾	cup (1½ sticks)softened butter
1	cup yellow cornmeal
6	tablespoons sugar
4	eggs
1½	cups all purpose flower
1	tablespoon baking powder
1½	teaspoons baking powder
1	can (15-ounce) cream style corn
1	can (4-ounce) diced green chilies
½	cup grated cheddar cheese, loosely packed
½	cup grated Monterey Jack cheese, loosely packed

Preheat oven to 375F and grease 9-inch square baking pan. Using an electric mixer, beat butter, cornmeal and sugar in a large bowl until well blended. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each additiona. Mix flour, baking powder and salt in a small bowl. Add to the batter and stir well. Mix in the corn, chilies, and both cheeses. Pour batter into the prepared pan. Bake until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 45 minutes. Cool slightly, cut into squares.
