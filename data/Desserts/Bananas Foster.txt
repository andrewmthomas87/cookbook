Bananas Foster

4 servings

4	ripe, firm bananas, peeled and sliced in half lengthwise
½	stick  unsalted butter
1	teaspoon vegetable oil
1	cup brown sugar
¼	cup banana liqueur
¼	teaspoon ground cinnamon
¼	cup dark rum
1	pint nonfat frozen vanilla yogurt

Peel and cut bananas in half lengthwise, then cut each piece into two equal pieces. Set aside.
In a large skillet over low heat, melt butter with oil. Stir in brown sugar, liquor and cinnamon. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes, stirring, or until the sugar has melted. Add the bananas and toss to coat well. Simmer for 5 minutes, or until the bananas are tender.
In a small saucepan, warm rum over low heat (do not boil). Carefully ignite the rum and pour over the bananas. Scoop frozen yogurt into 4 serving dishes and spoon the banana mixture over the top.
