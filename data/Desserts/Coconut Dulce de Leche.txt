Coconut Dulce de Leche

2½ cups

2	fourteen-ounce cans unsweetened coconut milk
1½	cups golden brown sugar (packed)
½	teaspoon coarse kosher salt

Whisk coconut milk, sugar, and salt in a heavy large skillet over medium heat until sugar dissolves. Increase heat to medium-high and boil until mixture is reduced to 2½  cups, stirring occasionally, about 20 minutes. Transfer sauce to small jars. Cool completely, then cover and chill. (Cam be made one month ahead. Keep chilled)
