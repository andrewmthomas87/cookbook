Lemon Cheesecake Squares

16 squares

Crust Ingredients:
8	regular graham crackers
2	tablespoons sugar
3	tablespoons butter, melted
Cheesecake Ingredients:
2	eight ounce packages cream cheese, room temperature
2	eggs
Â¾	cup sugar
1	lemon
2	tablespoons lemon zest
3	tablespoons lemon juice

Preheat oven to 325F. Line an 8x8 inch baking pan with aluminum foil so sides are hanging over.
In a food processor pulse graham crackers and sugar until finely ground. Add the butter and pulse until blended. Press into baking pan and bake for 10 to 12 minutes.
Meanwhile blend cream cheese until smooth. Add the eggs, sugar, lemon zest and juice. Blend well. Pour into hot crust, and bake for 30-35 minutes.
Let cool completely. Once cool, cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 2 hours up to 2 days. Lift out of pan, and cut into 16 squares.
