Molasses Cookies

about 3 dozen cookies

½	cup light molasses
1½	cups light brown sugar
1	cup melted butter
2	eggs
½	teaspoon baking soda
2	teaspoons ground cinnamon
½	teaspoon ground nutmeg
3	cups flour
½	cup milk
1½	cup raisins

Preheat the oven to 350F.
Beat the molasses, brown sugar, butter and the eggs together, then mix in the baking soda, cinnamon, and the nutmeg. Add the flour and milk in three separate parts, beating after each third is added. Mix in the raisins and distribute evenly through the dough.
Grease a cookie sheet, then drop the dough by the heaping teaspoonful to form the cookies. Bake for about 12 minutes.
