Pears & Marsala - Baked

6 servings

6	large firm pears (Red Bartlett or Anjou)
1	cup dry Marsala
1	cup sugar
1	stick cinnamon

Preheat oven to 325F. Peel, halve, and core the pears. Arrange the pear halves in a large baking dish, flat side up. Pour the Marsala over the pears and sprinkle with half of the sugar. Bake for 30 minutes, then turn the pears over with the flat side down.  Sprinkle with the rest of the sugar and bake for an additional 1Â½ to 2 hours. Baste and turn every 20 minutes.
