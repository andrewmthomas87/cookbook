Sausage Apple Bake - German Style

6-9 servings

6	Granny Smith or Jonathan apples, peeled, cored and thinly sliced
2	pounds pre-cooked bratwurst or kielbasa
2	teaspoons butter, or to tast
1	small onion, diced
2	medium potatoes, peeled, sliced thin
1	green bell pepper, seeded, diced
2	zucchini, sliced thin
1Â½	cups shredded mozzarella or Jack cheese (optional)

Peel, core and slice apples. Layer apples and sausage on the bottom of a 9x13x2-inch casserole dish and dot with butter. Then layer the onions, potatoes, bell peppers and zucchini. Bake in a preheated 300F oven for 1 hour. Once casserole is cooked, cover the top with cheese and bake until melted.
