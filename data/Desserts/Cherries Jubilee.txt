Cherries Jubilee

6-8 servings

½	cup granulated sugar
1	tablespoon cornstarch
¼	cup each water and orange juice
3	cups pitted Northwest fresh sweet cherries
½	teaspoon grated orange peel
¼	cup brandy, optional
1	quart vanilla ice cream

Combine sugar and cornstarch. Blend in water and orange juice. Cook and stir until thickened and smooth. Add cherries and orange peel; return to boil and simmer 10 minutes.
Gently heat brandy, pour over sauce and flame, if desired.
Serve over ice cream
