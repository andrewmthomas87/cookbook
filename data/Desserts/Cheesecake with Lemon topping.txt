Cheesecake with Lemon Topping

6-8 servings

Crust Ingredients:
1	cup graham cracker crumbs
8	tablespoons butter (1 stick)
½	cup sugar
Filling Ingredients
1½	cups cottage cheese
4	eggs
10	ounces sugar
¼	cup all purpose flour, sifted
2	eight ounce packages of cream cheese
juice & grated zest of one lemon
juice & grated zest of one lime
3	ounces unsalted butter, melted
1	cup of whipping cream
12	ounce jar of lemon curd

Put all of the ingredients for the crust in a food processor and process until combined. Press half of the mixture into the bottom of a greased 10 inch spring form pan and bake 10 minutes at 350F. Set aside to cool.
Mix the cottage cheese in the food processor for one minute. In an electric mixer mix the eggs and sugar until pale and thick. Then add the cottage cheese and cream cheese and mix until smooth. Sprinkle in the flour and the lemon and lime juices and zests and mix well followed by the whipping cream. Pour the mixture into the prepared spring-form pan and bake in the oven preheated to 300F for 2 hours. Set on a wire rack to cool, and then chill overnight. Turn the cheesecake out of the pan, spread the lemon curd on top and decorate with thin slices of lemon and lime.
