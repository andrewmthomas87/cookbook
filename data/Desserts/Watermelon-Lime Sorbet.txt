Watermelon-Lime Sorbet

6 servings

½	cup dry white wine
½	 cup sugar
¼	cup fresh lime juice
5	cups 1 inch cubes peeled seedless watermelon
2	tablespoons orange juice
Lime slices
Watermelon slices

Combine wine, sugar, and lime juice in heavy medium saucepan. Bring to simmer, stirring until sugar dissolves. Simmer gently for 3 minutes. Cool completely.
Puree cubed watermelon in food processor. Strain puree through sieve set over a large bowl, pressing on solids with the back of a rubber spatula. Discard the solids in the sieve. Stir wine mixture and orange juice concentrate into watermelon puree.
Transfer melon mixture to gelato or ice cream maker, process mixture according to machine instructions. Transfer to a container; cover and frees until firm, about 2 hours.
Freeze 6 small bowls for 30 minutes. Scoop sorbet into frozen bowls. Garnish with lime slices and watermelon wedges and serve immediately.
