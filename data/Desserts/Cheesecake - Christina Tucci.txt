Cheesecake - Christina Tucci

6-8 Servings

Crust
12	 Graham crackers
4	ounces butter
2	tablespoons brown sugar
Cinnamon & nutmeg
Filling
2	eggs
5	ounces sugar
2	eight ounce packages of cream cheese
Â¼	pint whipping cream
1	teaspoon vanilla
Cranberries

Make crust by melting butter with brown sugar. Crush Graham crackers, add cinnamon & nutmeg, and then mix with butter. Press into the bottom of a 10 inch spring form pan and bake 10 minutes at 350F. Set aside.
Whisk eggs and sugar until thick. Add cream cheese and whisk until smooth. Whisk cream and add to cheese mixture. Add vanilla and cranberries and mix. Pour mixture into crust and bake for 30 minutes, or until set.
