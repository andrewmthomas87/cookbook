Ice Cream with Sambuco Romano & Ground Espresso

4 servings

4	dessert dishes of vanilla ice cream
8	tablespoons Sambuco Romano
4	teaspoons finely ground espresso beans

Prepare four dessert dishes with 2 or 3 scoops of vanilla ice cream. Pour about 2 tablespoons of Sambuco Romano over each the ice cream in each dish. Sprinkle about 1 teaspoon of the finely ground expresso beans over each dish. Serve immediately.
