Apple Bread Pudding

4 servings

3	apples - peeled, cored and diced
3	cups bread crumbs
⅓	cup white sugar
½	cup raisins
¼	teaspoon ground nutmeg
¼	 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3	eggs, beaten
½	stick butter, melted
Juice of ½ lemon

Preheat oven to 350F.
In a large mixing bowl, combine the apples, bread crumbs and sugar. Add currants, nutmeg and cinnamon; mix well. Add the eggs; stir gently to combine. Add the melted butter and lemon juice and mix well.
Divide mixture evenly among buttered ramekins or put in a buttered soufflé dish.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, or until solid and golden brown.
