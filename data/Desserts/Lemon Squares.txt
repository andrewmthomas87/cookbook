Lemon Squares

8 servings

2	cups flour
½	cups powdered sugar
1	cup butter
4	eggs
2	cups sugar
⅓	cup fresh lemon juice
¼	cup flour
½	teaspoon baking powder

Preheat oven to 350F.
Crust: Blend flour, powdered sugar and butter thoroughly. Press mixture into the bottom of a 13x9 pan. Bake for 20 minutes (watch closely towards the end so the crust doesn’t burn!).
Filling:Beat eggs, sugar and lemon juice together. Add flour and baking powder, blending only long enough to incorporate.
Pour filling into baked crust and finish baking for 25 minutes until set.
Cool. Dust with powdered sugar before cutting.
