Apricot Tart Pepin

4 servings

1	flour tortilla -
1	to 2 cans of apricots
butter and sugar

Butter and sugar both sides of the tortilla and place on parchment paper on a cookie sheet.  Arrange apricot halves in a decorative pattern on top and put a few bits of butter over them. Bake at 400F for 20 minutes.
While the tart is cooking, simmer the syrup from the can on medium heat to make a glaze by reducing it. When the tart is done, carefully pour or with a silicon brush coat the tart with the glaze.
