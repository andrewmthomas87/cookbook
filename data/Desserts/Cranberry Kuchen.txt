Cranberry Kuchen

6 to 8 servings

Topping:
½	pound fresh cranberries
½	cup. sugar
¼	cup water
Juice of 1 orange
Batter:
⅔	cup unsalted butter, room temperature (1⅓ sticks)
½	cup sugar
2	eggs
1½	cup flour
1½	teaspoon baking powder
½	cup milk
2	tablespoons sour cream
Grated rind of 1 orange
Fresh nutmeg to taste

Preheat oven to 350F. Butter and flour a 2 quart soufflé dish.
In a small saucepan combine all topping ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and cook until the cranberries are soft, about 10 minutes. Set aside.
In a mixing bowl cream the butter and sugar for the batter with a mixer. Add eggs, one at a time, stirring well. Sift together the flour and baking powder. Stir flour into the batter mixture, a little at a time, alternating with the milk and sour cream. Stir in the orange rind and nutmeg.
Pour the batter into the prepared soufflé dish and top with the cranberry mixture. Bake about 60 minutes, or until golden brown and a long wooden skewer comes out clean when inserted to the bottom, sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve with vanilla ice cream.
