Coconut Pie

6-8 servings

4	tablespoons (½ stick) butter, melted
2	eggs, beaten
1	tablespoon all-purpose flour
¾	cup sugar
1	can (3½-ounce) shredded sweetened coconut (about 1 cup)
1	 cup milk
1	unbaked pie shell (9-inch)

Preheat oven to 350F. In a large bowl, combine melted butter, eggs, flour, sugar, coconut, and milk. Pour into pie shell. Bake until firm, about 45 to 60 minutes.
