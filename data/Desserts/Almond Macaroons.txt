Almond Macaroons

4 servings

3	large egg whites
Zest of 1 large lemon
1	cup sugar
1Â½	cups blanched finely ground almonds

Beat the egg whites until they form soft peaks. Mix in zest, and then sugar. Fold in almonds.
Cover a baking sheet with aluminum foil, then grease it. Scoop 2 teaspoons of the batter per cookie onto the sheet. Bake for 30 minutes at 275F. Let cool.
