Blueberry Kuchen

12 servings

1½	cups all-purpose flour
¾	cup sugar
2	teaspoons baking powder
1½	teaspoons grated lemon peel
½	teaspoon ground nutmeg
¼	teaspoon salt
⅔	cup milk
¼	cup butter, melted
1	egg, beaten
1	teaspoon vanilla extract
2	cups fresh or frozen blueberries
Topping:
¾	cup sugar
½	cup all-purpose flour
¼	cup butter, melted

In a bowl, combine the first six ingredients. Add the milk, butter, egg and vanilla. Beat for 2 minutes or until well blended. Pour into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking dish. Sprinkle with blueberries.
In a bowl, combine sugar and flour; add butter. Toss with a fork until crumbly; sprinkle over blueberries.
Bake at 350F for 40 minutes or until lightly browned.
