Berries with Mascarpone Cream

4-6 servings

1	quart strawberries, hulled, thinly sliced
½	pint each: fresh blueberries, fresh raspberries
⅓ cup plus 1 tablespoon confectioners' sugar
4	ounces mascarpone, softened
1	tablespoon half-and-half
2	tablespoons limoncello (lemon liqueur) or orange-flavored liqueur

Mix berries and ⅓ cup of the sugar in a serving bowl; let stand a few minutes, stirring occasionally.
Mix remaining 1 tablespoon of sugar, mascarpone, half-and-half and liqueur in a small bowl until smooth. Serve berries in small dishes with a dollop of the mascarpone mixture.
