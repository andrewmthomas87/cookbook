Banana Ice Cream

1 quart

6	bananas - frozen
1½	cups heavy cream
¾	cup light corn syrup
1	teaspoon vanilla extract
1	tablespoon lemon juice

Thaw frozen bananas for 45 minutes, then remove pulp and mix in a food processor with the other ingredients. Strain through a sieve; then put in the gelato maker and churn until semihard. Put in container and freeze until ready to serve.
