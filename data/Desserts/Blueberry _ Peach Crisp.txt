Blueberry & Peach Crisp

6-8 servings

1	cup sifted flour
½	cup brown sugar
¼	cup butter (1/2 stick)
1	teaspoon baking powder
½	teaspoon salt
2	cups fresh blueberries
3	cups fresh peaches, peeled, sliced
¼	cup granulated sugar
½	teaspoon cinnamon

Preheat oven to 375F. Combine first 5 ingredients in a food processor and mix until crumbly. Mix blueberries & peaches with the granulated sugar and the cinnamon, then place in a 2 quart buttered baking dish; sprinkle with the flour mixture. Bake about 40 minutes or until the top is browned. Let set for at least 10 minutes, and then serve at room temperature with ice cream or whipped cream.
