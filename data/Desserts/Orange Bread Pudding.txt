Orange Bread Pudding

4 servings

1	cup bread cubes cut about ½ inch each (challah or egg bread, crusts removed)
¾	cup scalded milk
¾	cup orange juice
grated rind of 1 large orange
2	large eggs, beaten
½	cup sugar
⅛	teaspoon salt
butter
Vanilla ice cream

Preheat oven to 375F. Soak the bread cubes in the scalded milk for ten minutes. In a separate Combine the orange juice and grated rind, eggs, sugar, and salt in a separate mixing bowl. Stir until the sugar is dissolved, then add to the bread and milk mixture.
Pour into four well-buttered individual ramekins, dot with butter, and place the ramekins into a flat pan of hot water. Bake for thirty minutes, or until a toothpick inserted one inch from the outside edge comes away clean. Serve with vanilla ice cream.
